**Note**: 
- **[IFeatureNameFields](/api-reference/net/IFeatureNameFields/)** is equivalent to **IStreetNameFields** and **IStreetNameFields2** in ArcObjects SDK. 
- **[FeatureNameFields](/api-reference/net/FeatureNameFields/)** is equivalent to **StreetNameFields** in ArcObjects SDK.
- **[INetworkForwardStar](/api-reference/net/INetworkForwardStar/)** implements the equivalent methods of both **INetworkForwardStar** and **INetworkForwardStarEx** in ArcObjects SDK.
- **[INetworkSourceDirections](/api-reference/net/INetworkSourceDirections/)** implements the equivalent methods of **INetworkSourceDirections**, **ISignposts**, and **IRoadSplits** in ArcObjects SDK.

If you migrate your ArcObjects SOE or SOI project that references the above ArcObjects interfaces or coclass, make sure you use their equivalent APIs in ArcGIS Enterprise SDK. See more details in [Migration Strategies](../migration-strategies-net/).
